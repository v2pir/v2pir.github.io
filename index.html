About the creator

Vansh Chhabra was always interested in making cool things. He would tinker around with small circuits and makes small fans and other things. He also loved reading comic books. Then he read a comic book about robots. That changed everything for him. He learned that everything does not rely on physics. He started to learn Python. He had originally started coding in 3rd grade, learning Scratch, but that was only because his school made him. He had learned python for 2 years until he started HTML. He was in 9th grade when he built this website.

Why this website?

When Vansh was learning Python, he came across many struggles. He would find bugs and they would give him a headache, just trying to find where the bug is. He would look online at already made codes and he would see how the programmer of those codes wrote those programs. Eventually, he started to learn how to identify a bug and where to look for it. He learned what the errors meant and how to fix them. This fascinated him. He decided to help other individuals learn code as well, hoping others would get as much out of it as he did.
